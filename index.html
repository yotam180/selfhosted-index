Welcome to SelfHosted

What is this?

SelfHosted is our private "cloud" platform. We use it to host our web applications, servers, APIs and services so they are accessible by the public.

You can browse the different services by the subdomains. For instance;

  • werewolf.selfhosted.website
  • minecraft.selfhosted.website

Can I host with SelfHosted?

No, get your own SelfHosted. This is our SelfHosted.
